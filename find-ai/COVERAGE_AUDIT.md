## Real Gaps (Top 100 shortfall — ~35%)

1. **Fire safety & Bomba compliance** — major landlord liability
2. **Inheritance / probate of tenanted property** — landlord dies, tenancy continues?
3. **Divorce & matrimonial home** — spouse blocks sale, co-owner conflict
4. **Flood / natural disaster** — Ganu + KL flash flood, insurance, tenancy suspension, rent relief
5. **Drugs / criminal activity in rental** — Dangerous Drugs Act s.39B forfeiture risk
6. **Tenant death in property** — cleaning, estate, deposit, police
7. **CCRIS / CTOS dispute** — wrongly listed tenant, correction process
8. **Accessibility compliance** — PWD Act 2008, UBBL ramps/lifts
9. **Co-owner deadlock** — one wants to sell, others won't → partition action
10. **Tenancy fraud** — fake IC, forged payslips, syndicates
