# Pilot Landlord Onboarding — what we send them after they confirm

> **Purpose:** A single message (WhatsApp / email) sent to each pilot landlord once they've confirmed interest. Sets expectations clearly so they walk through the v0 mock with the right mindset.
>
> Last updated: 2026-04-25 (v3.4.6)

## Optional walkthrough call (for high-value pilots)

For pilots who prefer a guided walkthrough (older landlords, agents managing many properties, anyone visibly tech-uncomfortable):

**Offer:** "Happy to walk through it with you on a 20-min Zoom call — I share my screen, you give live reactions. No prep needed, just let me know a time that works."

**Call structure:**
- 2 min — Welcome, frame the pilot (mock, feedback ask, no real data)
- 10 min — Walk them through the Screen tile end-to-end, narrate what you're showing, pause at decision points to ask "what would you do here?"
- 5 min — Open Q&A while it's fresh
- 3 min — Send them the feedback form to fill async, thank them

Capture call notes in the pilot tracking sheet.
