Why this is better
Separation of concerns

Landing's job = earn the click. Chat's job = pick a tool or ask a question. When you make the landing also be the toolkit, you slow down the click AND weaken the empty state. By splitting, both pages do one thing excellently. This is how Apple, Linear, Notion, Stripe, and Claude all structure their entry flows.

The bento already exists in your chat empty state (page.js lines ~1914-2071 from this session's v2 ship). We just delete it from landing.js and let the existing empty state shine.
