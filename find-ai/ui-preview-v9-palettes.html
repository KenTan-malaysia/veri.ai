Zeus's recommendation
Option A · Warm Navy Trust

Why: the cream canvas signals warmth and unhurried trust — like a lawyer's office that still serves you tea. Deep sea navy ink is bank-level serious but softer than true black. Warm gold is used once (underline on "let's make sure it's safe") so it reads as a seal of quality, not decoration. It respects CLAUDE.md doctrine (Mature Minimalism, navy + charcoal + crisp whitespace, no cartoon startup green) while adding the Malaysian hospitality warmth your users actually want when they're nervous about signing.

Option B (Paper Zen) is beautiful but reads as upmarket wabi-sabi — wrong for a first-time accidental landlord. Option C (Ocean Teal) is clean and fintech-modern but feels like a bank app, not a trusted advisor. Warm Navy Trust threads the needle.
